Deal or No Deal

https://leopoldomanzo.github.io/DealOrNoDealWebGL/

Deal or No Deal (https://leopoldomanzo.github.io/DealOrNoDealWebGL/) is a 2 dimensional video game built using C# in the Unity engine. The construct of the game consists of the same rules as the classic game show: Throughout the game, the player is offered an amount of money or prizes to quit, being asked the titular question, "Deal or no deal?" If the contestant rejects every deal and eliminates all the other cases or boxes, the player keeps the money that was in the original case or box. As a fun spin on the game, I was able to add fun imagery and music to replicate a space / U.F.O theme.
