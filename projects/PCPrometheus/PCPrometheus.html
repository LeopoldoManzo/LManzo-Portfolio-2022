P.C. Prometheus

http://pcprometheus.com/

P.C. Prometheus(http://pcprometheus.com/) is a website developed by a team of 4 developers including myself, which launched in May of 2020. At P.C. Prometheus, we believe that everyone has the ability to learn more about the ins-and-outs of computers. We made this website targeted at all audiences, whether you're a pro or not, to construct your own computer. The construct of this project took over 6 months to get to fully functional and has been updated as late as winter of 2021. The website is developed in Angular and includes advanced features such as API scoping Amazon to give accurate results for filtering products, along with a technical algorythym for the quiz section of the website.
