San Joaquin A+

https://sjaplus.org/

San Joaquin A+(https://sjaplus.org/) collaborates with local families, education, and workforce partners to set a vision for how our community can prepare students to be successful and responsible citizens. I was given the duty of rebuilding the homepage for this website in collaboration with a UX/UI designer and a project manager during my internship at CodeStack. The project was my first experience working in a professional environment with deadlines and high quality expectations. In order to complete the re-design of the homepage, I first created a prototype using Adobe XD trying to find the right color scheme and best layout for all of the content. Once I got that approved from the UX/UI designer, I presented the prototype with the project manager to the company and once approved, moved forward with re-designing the webpage using Angular and TypeScript along with SCSS to replicate the prototype into the live homepage.
