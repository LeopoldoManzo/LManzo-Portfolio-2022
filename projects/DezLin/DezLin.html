DezLin LLC.

https://www.dezlin.com

DezLin LLC. (https://www.dezlin.com) is a retail + wholesale manufacturing company based in Lodi California. I was hired in June of 2021 to rebuild their company website. Originally, they had a previous web team who built their website using WordPress, my job was to first re-design the wordpress site to have functional features while I transfered all store data to a Shopify website where we would then completely re-design the layout and functionality of the everything including but not limited to: ensuring the website was responsive and had fast loading speeds, creating an accessible platform for all impaired visitors, having the ability to distinguish retail and wholesale customers based on login and displaying correct product pricing based on customer status, adding the option of an affiliate program, and much more. The skills required involed having a strong understanding of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and Liquid. Along with that, I had to understand how to work with ShipStation to handle inventory management. I also did minor work for the company using CommentSold, a live selling platform.
